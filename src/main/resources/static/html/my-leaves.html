Leave Policy

  • During Probation: Leave balance is ZERO. All leaves are unpaid and will be deducted from salary.
  • After Probation: You earn 1.5 leaves per month (9 leaves per 6-month cycle)
  • Maximum 3 paid leaves can be taken at one time
  • Excess days beyond 3 will be converted to unpaid leave
  • Up to 10 leaves can be carried forward to next cycle (Jan-Jun, Jul-Dec)
  • At cycle end, remaining leaves expire and new cycle starts

⚠️ During Probation: You are earning leaves but they are FROZEN until probation ends. All current leaves are unpaid and will be deducted from salary. The "Paid" column shows N/A, and "Unpaid" column shows days that will be deducted.
